Key Features

Tree Tracking

Log your planted trees and track their growth over time with regular check-ins.

Activity Streaks

Maintain daily streaks by walking to check on your trees, improving both environmental and personal health.

Progress Analytics

Visualize your impact with detailed statistics on trees planted, steps taken, and CO2 offset.

Community

Share your journey, get inspired by others, and receive meaningful recognition for your efforts.

How Arogyavan Works

1

Plant & Register

Plant a tree in your community or garden and register it in the app with location and details.

2

Daily Check-ins

Walk to your tree daily to check its health and log your visit in the app to maintain your streak.

3

Track Progress

Monitor your tree's growth and your health improvements through our analytics dashboard.

4

Share & Inspire

Share your journey with the community and inspire others to join the movement.
